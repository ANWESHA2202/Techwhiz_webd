Coding


Rules & Instructions

Individual Event
Number of judges: 2
Estimated time : 2.5 hours

1.It will be a coding event consisting of 2 rounds.
2.First round will be a qualifying quiz.
3.Second round will be conducted on meet and will consist of 5 sets of 3 questions each.
4.Each set of questions will be displayed for 15 minutes.
5.Participants will have to solve the given problems within the 15 min time frame. Maximun 5 minute delay will be allowed in mailing the solution codes to the given mail, after which they will not be considered.
6.Participant with most number of correct solutions wins.
7.In case of a tie, participant who has attempted questions from maximum number of sets will be declared winner. Time of submission will also be taken account of.
